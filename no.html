Rian Movies

Manamey

"Manamey" is a Telugu romantic-comedy film directed by Sriram Adittya, starring Sharwanand and Krithi Shetty. Set in London, the story follows Vikram, a carefree young man, who unexpectedly becomes a guardian to a child. The film balances humor and emotion with vibrant visuals and a soothing score.

Qalb

"Qalb" is a 2024 Malayalam romantic drama directed by Sajid Yahiya. It follows Leonardo “Calpo” and Thumbi through the Sufi stages of love. The film offers poetic storytelling, stunning visuals, and a philosophical touch.

Friendship

"Friendship" (2021) is a Tamil campus drama starring Harbhajan Singh. It explores college life, friendship, and a fight for justice following a tragedy. The film blends sports, hostel culture, and courtroom drama.

Emoji

"Emoji" is a 2024 Telugu romantic comedy directed by Sandeep Raj. It follows Karthik and Anika as they navigate love and modern relationships. The film is light-hearted with catchy music and relatable emotions.

Malli Rava

"Malli Rava" is a 2017 Telugu romantic drama starring Sumanth and Aakanksha Singh. It tells the story of Karthik and Anjali, childhood friends reunited by fate. The film explores themes of destiny and enduring love.
