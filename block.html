Manamey

"Manamey"is a Telugu romantic-comedy film directed by Sriram Adittya, starring Sharwanand and Krithi Shetty in lead roles. Set in London, the story follows Vikram, a carefree young man, who is unexpectedly thrust into the role of a guardian for a child named Kushi after a tragic loss. Alongside Subhadra, a responsible and grounded woman, the two navigate the challenges of co-parenting while their relationship gradually blossoms into love. The film balances humor and emotion, offering a visually rich experience with vibrant cinematography and a soothing musical score. While the first half is praised for its heartwarming moments and sharp writing, the second half tends to slow down with predictable elements. Nevertheless, "Manamey" succeeds as a light-hearted, feel-good entertainer with strong performances and family appeal.

QALB

"Qalb" is a 2024 Malayalam romantic drama directed by Sajid Yahiya, featuring Ranjith Sajeev and Neha Nazneen in lead roles. Set in the serene landscapes of Alappuzha, the film follows the emotional journey of Leonardo “Calpo” Calpo, a carefree café owner, and Thumbi, a quiet woman from a traditional background. Their love story is uniquely structured around the seven Sufi stages of love—attraction, infatuation, love, trust, worship, obsession, and death—blending spiritual depth with poetic storytelling. With stunning visuals, soulful music, and heartfelt performances, "Qalb" explores the intensity of love and loss. While it has been praised for its emotional resonance and aesthetic beauty, some critics noted a predictable screenplay and occasional pacing issues. Despite mixed reviews, the film touched many viewers with its philosophical undertone and emotional depth.

Friendship

"Friendship" (2021) is a Tamil campus drama directed by John Paul Raj and Sham Surya, marking cricketer Harbhajan Singh’s debut as a college student. The film centers on a tight-knit group of friends whose bond is tested when they band together to seek justice for a female classmate who has been brutally assaulted and killed. While the movie follows familiar tropes—college pranks, hostel life, cricket matches—it shifts dramatically midway into courtroom proceedings. Despite strong chemistry among the ensemble and the novelty of Harbhajan’s casting, critics described it as a dreary, unfocused watch with uneven pacing, underdeveloped characters, and subpar performances—though its central theme of loyalty shines through .

Emoji

"Emoji" is a 2024 Telugu romantic comedy directed by Sandeep Raj, featuring Nithiin and Anupama Parameswaran in lead roles. The film follows the story of a young couple, Karthik and Anika, who navigate the ups and downs of their relationship while dealing with societal pressures and personal insecurities. Set against the backdrop of vibrant city life, "Emoji" explores themes of love, friendship, and self-discovery through a blend of humor and emotion. The film is praised for its engaging screenplay, catchy music, and strong performances by the lead actors. With its relatable characters and heartwarming moments, "Emoji" aims to resonate with audiences looking for a light-hearted yet meaningful cinematic experience.

Malli Rava

"Malli Rava" is a 2017 Telugu romantic drama directed by Gowtam Tinnanuri, featuring Sumanth and Aakanksha Singh in lead roles. The film tells the story of two individuals, Karthik and Anjali, who meet during their childhood and develop a deep bond that transcends time and distance. As they grow older, their paths diverge, but fate brings them back together, forcing them to confront their feelings and the choices they have made. With its poignant storytelling, beautiful cinematography, and soulful music, "Malli Rava" explores themes of love, destiny, and the enduring power of relationships. The film received positive reviews for its emotional depth and strong performances by the lead actors.
